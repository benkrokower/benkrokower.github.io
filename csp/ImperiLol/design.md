# 1. Introduction to the Collatz Conjecture
The Collatz Conjecture, otherwise known as Kakutani's Conjecture, Ulan's Conjecture, The Syracuse Problem or simply 3n + 1 is one of the most famous unsolved problems in all of mathematics. The problem is as follows:

Start with a number, if its even you **divide** by 2, but if its odd you **Multiply** by 3 and then *Add* 1. Repeat for the New Number

The Conjecture asks that **with enough repetition, do all positive integers converge to 1?**

It is named after German Mathematician Lothar Collatz

# 2. Content Outline
My Website will be a calculator for the Collatz Function, it is a simple mathematical operation applied to positive integers (no words or negatives.) It determines the next number in a sequence based on whether the current number is even or odd and then applies the corelating steps.
