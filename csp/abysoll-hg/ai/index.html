About the Game

Circle Clicker is a fast-paced arcade game where you click 100 circles as they move across the screen. The faster you click and the more accurate you are, the higher your score!

How to Play

Goal: Click all 100 circles before time runs out.

  • Circles appear randomly on the screen
  • Click each circle as quickly as possible
  • Aim for 100% accuracy
  • Watch your score and accuracy climb!

High Scores & Challenge

Can you beat the game with perfect accuracy? Try to click all 100 circles without missing a single one!
