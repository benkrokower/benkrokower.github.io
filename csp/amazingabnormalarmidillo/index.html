🍰 About the Game

An's Cakeria is a cozy cake decorating game where you get to be the pastry chef! Choose from a variety of frostings, toppings, and decorations to build your perfect cake. Whether you want something elegant, whimsical, or totally wild — the choice is yours!

🎮 How to Play

  • Click on frosting colors to change the frosting
  • Click on cake flavors to change the flavor
  • Drag toppings onto your cake
  • Press done to finish your cake to receive a surprise
  • Have fun and experiment!
  • Compare with friends!
  • Make different variations!
