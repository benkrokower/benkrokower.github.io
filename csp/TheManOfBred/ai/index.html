📋 About the Game

This is the classic 2 player game where X and O take turns filling in squares in the grid. Strategize your moves to get three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to win!

🎯 What You'll Love

Simple rules, endless strategy. Whether you're a casual player or a competitive enthusiast, Tic Tac Toe delivers quick, exciting matches that keep you coming back for more!

⚡ Quick & Engaging

No downloads needed. No complicated mechanics. Just pure, classic gameplay that's been entertaining players for generations. Play anytime, anywhere!

📖 How to Play

  • Players: 2 players per game (X and O)
  • Goal: Get three of your symbols in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
  • Turns: Players take turns clicking on empty squares
  • Blocking: Block your opponent's winning moves strategically
  • Win: First player to get three in a row wins!
  • Draw: If all squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw
