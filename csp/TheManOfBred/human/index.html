Tic Tac Toe

This is the classic 2 player game.

Learn more about Tic Tac Toe!

Some other names for Tic Tac Toe are:

  • Noughts and Crosses
  • X's and O's

The gameplay loop goes as follows:

  1. X (player 1) clicks in a square to play
  2. O (Player 2) clicks in a square to play
