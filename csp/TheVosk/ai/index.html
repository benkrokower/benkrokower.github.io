ABOUT THE GAME

The Cybird on the Run is a student-made arcade game developed for a game jam. Guide a cybernetic bird through a gauntlet of obstacles, enemies, and challenges in this high-speed platformer. Reflexes, timing, and a little bit of chaos are your only tools.

Built with passion by the team at OpenGrit Games, this project showcases what people can achieve in a few days. Every pixel, sound, and mechanic was crafted from scratch.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Click ▶ Play Now above to launch the game
  2. Press Space to jump or flap
  3. Avoid obstacles in your path
  4. Beat your high score and challenge friends!
