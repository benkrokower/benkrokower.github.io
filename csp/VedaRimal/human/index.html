Evil Asteroids


Evil Asteroids is a fast paced retro style game where the main goal is to defend your spaceship from the evil asteroids that are falling from space.

    These asteroids come in all different sizes
    You have to be precise with your shots.
    As you keep destroying the asteroids, they will start to get faster.
    The asteroids wont stop! keep destroying them and save your ship.

  • Use arrow keys to move
  • Use up arrow to shoot your lasers.
