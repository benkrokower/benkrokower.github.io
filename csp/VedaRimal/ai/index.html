About the Game

Control your spaceship and blast through waves of asteroids. Dodge incoming threats and rack up points. How long can you survive in the depths of space?

How to Play

  • Arrow Keys: Move your spaceship
  • Spacebar: Shoot lasers
  • Goal: Destroy asteroids and avoid collisions
  • Points: Gain points for each asteroid destroyed
