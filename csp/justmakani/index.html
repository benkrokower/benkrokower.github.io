About the Game

Mathman is an evil take on Hangman. Guess the wrong letter and you won't just lose a part — you'll need to solve a math problem. Are you up for it?

  • Evil word selection algorithm
  • Math penalties for wrong guesses
  • Endless replayability

How to Play

  1. Choose a letter you think is in the hidden word
  2. If you're wrong, you get a math equation
  3. Solve it correctly or lose a life
  4. Reveal every letter in the word to win
