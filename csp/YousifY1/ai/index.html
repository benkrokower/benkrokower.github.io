About the Game

The High-Five is a fun and interactive game where each high-five makes the stick figure progressively more upset. Can you handle the challenge? See how far you can push the stick figure's emotions!

How to Play

Controls:

  • Click the "High-Five" button to give a high-five
  • Watch the stick figure's reaction change
  • Try to reach the highest upset level!
