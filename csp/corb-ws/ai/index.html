About Connect 4

Connect 4 is a classic 2-player strategy game where players take turns dropping colored pieces into a grid. The first player to get 4 pieces in a row—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—wins the game!

Test your skills, predict your opponent's moves, and become a Connect 4 champion.

How to Play

1. Take Turns

Players alternate dropping pieces from the top of the board.

2. Choose Your Column

Click or tap the column where you want to drop your piece.

3. Get 4 in a Row

Be the first to connect 4 pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

4. Win the Game

Block your opponent and claim your victory!
