Connect 4

My game website

Connect 4 is a game where you have to connect four of your colored discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally while stopping your opponent from doing the same.

  • Players take turns dropping colored discs into the grid.
  • The first player to connect four discs in a row wins.
  • If the grid is full and no player has connected four discs, the game is a draw.
