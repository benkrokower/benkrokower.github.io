About the Game

Library Game is a deck building game where you build decks to be used by characters, fighting increasingly powerful enemies. Upon victory, you gain their cards to improve your characters and decks.

How to Play

Controls

  • Use mouse to click cards
  • Select cards to add to your deck
  • Battle enemies to earn new cards

Thourough Instructions

In this game you have characters, each of these characters have CARD SLOTS (the red blue box) By holding your mouse over the CARD SLOT you can see all of the cards available to the character If you click on the CARD SLOT it will cause the cards to continue to be displayed allowing you to select a card Above each character's CARD SLOT their current and maximum light is displayed by the yellow circles, each displayed card has a Light cost in the top left If you click on a card, a blue targeting line will appear allowing you to select opposing characters CARD SLOT As long as the character has enough Light, by clicking on a CARD SLOT on top of an opposing character, the page will be selected and targeted This can be seen by hovering the mouse over that CARD SLOT showing the selected card At the start of each turn, each character will draw 1 card from their deck and recover 1 light up to their max The lines coming out of each CARD SLOT display what type of interaction the card will have Red and Blue lines mean that the selected card is uncontested, Yellow lines mean the page is Clashing with the page on the opposing CARD SLOT When pages Clash the first die on both pages will be rolled and then compared based on their type If two red combat dice are rolled against each other, the higher number wins dealing damage equal to the result and modifiers If a red combat die is rolled against a blue defensive die and that blue defensive die has the Evade symbol, if the Evade die wins the character regains stagger and the Evade die will be reused If the Evade die loses the character will take full damage Alternatively if a red combat die is rolled against a blue defensive die and that blue defensive die has the Block symbol, damage will be reduced by the Block roll If the Block die is greater, the attacker will take stagger damage equal to the difference in die values If a character takes damage, they lose red health and yellow stagger influenced by the resistance table Fatal means they will take 2x damage, Weak means they will take 1.5x damage, normal means they will take 1x damage Endured means they will take .5x damage, Ineffective means they will take .25x damage, Immune means they will take 0x damage Once a character runs out of their yellow stagger bar they will become staggered, preventing them from acting and set all of their resistances to fatal for a turn Staggering a character will restore 1 light to the attacking character, Once a character runs out of their red health bar, they will die Above each character there is an emotion bar, This bar fills with positive and negative emotions up to the floor max emotion level starting at 0 Whenever a character's emotion levels up they regain all light, At a high enough emotion level they will unlock extra CARD SLOTS
