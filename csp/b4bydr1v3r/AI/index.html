About the Game

Welcome to Lando Click — a fun, interactive game where you click on Lando Norris's logo to show your support for the McLaren Formula 1 driver!

Every click represents your cheers during race weekends. The more you click, the more you celebrate your favorite driver. It's simple, it's fun, and it's all about showing Lando some love!

🎮
⚙️

How to Play

The rules are simple:

  • Click on Lando's logo to cheer for him
  • See the McLaren team colors light up with your support
  • Share the game with other F1 fans!
