⚔️ About the Game

Welcome to the desert! This is a Turn Based Attack Game you command a squad of three character through sands and ancient ruins.

The Goal? Collect resources, upgrade your gear, battle through 10 levels, and defeat the final boss! Strategy is required.

Are you ready? The desert awaits...

🌵 🏜️ 🦂 ☀️ 🌵
  • Control three characters with special abilities
  • Gather resources to upgrade your tools
  • Battle through 10 challenging levels
  • Upgrade weapons, armor, and gear
  • Face thefinal boss on Level 10!

🎮 How to Play

Here's everything you need to know:

🖱️

Select Characters

Click on your characters to select them and view their abilities and stats.

⚔️

Attack Enemies

Choose your targets wisely! Each enemy has different strengths and weaknesses.

🎒

Collect Resources

Gather gold, weapons, and items scattered across each level to grow stronger.

⬆️

Upgrade Gear

Visit the shop between levels to buy powerful upgrades for your squad.

🧠

Plan Your Strategy

Think ahead! Timing is needed to to win.

👑

Beat the Boss

Survive all 10 levels and take down the final boss to win the game!
