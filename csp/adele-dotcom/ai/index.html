About the Game

Welcome to the Sorting Game! This fun puzzle game challenges you to sort blocks by placing them in the correct holes. The faster you sort, the more points you earn!

How to Play

  • Drag blocks to match them with holes
  • Each correct match earns you points
  • Complete all blocks to finish the level
  • Try to beat your high score!
